About Mummysport

I am a dynamic Nigerian athlete, educator, and sports writer from the University of Ilorin, recognized for achievements in track and field and my work as a certified exercise therapist and content creator focused on fitness, wellness, and empowering others through sports.

Through my "Mummysport" persona, I merge fitness with inspiration, creating impactful content and mentoring young students and professionals in sports and personal development.

My mission is to guide the younger generation to embrace fitness, develop holistically, and use their skills for positive impact in Nigeria's health and education sectors.
